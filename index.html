Resy is a restaurant reservations and marketing platform, where premium diners get premium tables via our mobile app. We don't think that you should have to plan 30 days in advance to get a primetime reservation. So, we offer great seats at great restaurants on short notice, confirmed instantly. If you love restaurants, it’s addictive.

Here you'll find libraries and documentation about the Resy platform as well as open source software that we've contributed. We're dedicated to providing high quality software and APIs that allow you to integrate the Resy platform into your application.

To obtain an API key for the Resy API, email us at api@resy.com.
